Problem Statement ("Sound Familiar?")

Headline: Most businesses waste growth potential because the system behind the result is weak.

Many businesses do not actually have an advertising strategy. They have spend. Campaigns launch without a clear offer hierarchy, creative testing is weak, landing pages are misaligned, and decision-making depends too much on platform dashboards in isolation. That creates familiar symptoms: high CAC, unstable performance, weak lead quality, poor attribution confidence, and no reliable framework for scaling.

How It Works (4-Step Scale Logic)
  1. Audit and Baseline: We review existing accounts, tracking, and unit economics to locate performance blockages.

  3. Launch and Optimise: Launch structure-driven campaigns with systematic, weekly creative variation testing.
  4. Scale With Control: Step up spend based on customer acquisition cost parameters and strict contribution margins.

Section 07

Developer Build & SEO Instructions

Structural directions for your front-end team or design editor.

1. Clean SEO Heading Structure

Ensure exactly one semantic H1 per page to hold target performance copy. Sub-headers use modular nested H2 and H3 without using bold stylings purely to visually simulate tags.

2. Speed & Fast Image Loading

High-intent ad landers will bleed up to 20% of traffic for every 1-second delay. Compress landing visuals, use WebP formats, and avoid scripts blocking rendering above the fold.

3. Responsive Breakpoint Anchors

Ensure forms stay readable and easily clickable across mobile widths (min-touch area 44px). Buttons must stack vertically on smartphone displays instead of shrinking text.
